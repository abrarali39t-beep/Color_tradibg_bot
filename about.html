Who We Are

Your Kashmir Family

At Aadi Tour & Travel, we believe Kashmir is not just a destination — it's a feeling. We are a team of passionate, born-and-raised Kashmiri travel professionals based in Pampore, J&K.

Since 2020, we have helped over 500 families, couples, and groups experience the real Kashmir — from the lotus-dotted Dal Lake to the powdery slopes of Gulmarg, the meadows of Pahalgam to the glaciers of Sonmarg.
